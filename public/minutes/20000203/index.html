Meeting Minutes – 02/03/2000

Thursday, February 3, 2000 Minutes by Zaphod

Members Present: oz, pacd, zaphod, tmiley, magoo, phorceph

Order of Business:

  1. Unitrac possibly willing to dontate laptops to the club for tax credit
