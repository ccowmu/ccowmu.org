Start: 6:22pm

Members Present: Zaphod, Pacd, Rattles, Cheddar, Bob, ZRodger, Mike, Eclipse, Mercy, NeoKnite

  1. We need to figure out the rules for getting our money. We need to make sure when we buy the stuff we get reimbursed. Wes says he will try speaking to Bonny sometime next week.

  8. Elections are still in the air. Nominations are welcome.

  11. Matt showed us what happens if you telnet to bofh.jive.org, port 666.
