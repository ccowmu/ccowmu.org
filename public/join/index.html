Join the Computer Club

Become part of Western Michigan University's premier technical organization.

Why Join the Computer Club?

With our own office and the resources in it, our greatest resource remains our people. After almost 50 years at WMU, we've gained hundreds of great members doing remarkable things at companies like Google, Apple, LinkedIn, IBM, and the DoD.

If you're even a little interested in security, programming, hardware, electronics, or have an idea for your own project, then chances are you'll find a friend in us.

The best way to see if we're the right fit is stopping by a meeting to see for yourself. Check out our meeting minutes to see what a typical meeting might entail.

Membership Requirements

Enrollment: WMU student (any major welcome)
Dues: $10 per academic year
Meetings: Thursdays 6:00 PM
Location: 2225 Kohrman Hall

How to Join

1

Attend a Meeting

Come to any Thursday meeting at 6:00 PM in 2225 Kohrman Hall

2

Pay Dues

Annual membership: $10

Cash: Bring $10 to any meeting

PayPal: rso_cclub@wmich.edu

3

Create Account

We'll help you set up chat access and add you to our systems

Member Benefits

Technical Workshops

Hands-on learning sessions covering programming languages, development tools, and emerging technologies

Project Collaboration

Work on real-world projects with fellow members, building portfolio-worthy applications and systems

Industry Connections

Network with alumni working at major tech companies and attend industry speaker events

Hardware Access

Use club equipment including servers, development boards, sensors, and testing equipment

Competition Teams

Participate in game jams, hackathons, and cybersecurity competitions

Professional Development

Resume reviews, interview preparation, and career guidance from experienced members
