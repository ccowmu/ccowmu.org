Members present: Tim Miley, Steve Sedore, Jeremy Jubenville, Wesley Leonard, Paul Miller, Dave Bocek, Dennis Huppertz, Darron Christian, Chris Nicholson, James Weng, Karl Sebek, James Nelli.

2) Officers: we need new officers to take over for next year. Steve and Dennis are leaving, Darron and Tim have done everything already. Basically, we are creating officer training program for CCLUB. We are looking for recruits or getting more involved will be asked to talk to Steve and help them with questions. Try to get people interested in certain position and get them acclimatized to their responsibility and insure continuity. The more you do, the more confident you get.

People who are interested (at least two) must be full time students. Pres must be full time students, plus one other officer. If you are interested, email steve. Recruit, recruit, recruit. The club will fail if we don't get new officers this year. it is THAT BAD!!!

We have about 100 members, but we get about 5 or 6 people at meetings. Many members are alumni, but still large numbers of people would be great. W would like to be the largest organization on campus.

Tim: let's snail mail all CS people. Probably no more that $0.20. i

Gives information useful for both club members and class. The unix part is crucial.
