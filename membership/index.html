Membership

Just head down to Kohrman Hall, Room 2225 during office hours or on any Thursday at 6pm. A one-year membership costs $10 and gets you:

  • Awesome Job Networking
  • Standing invite to compete on our Ctf Team
  • An account on our Club server, complete with SSH privileges
  • Web hosting
  • Discounts on entry to PLAN, our Bi-Annual LANparty
  • Access to our tools, servers, and educational resources

What is really offered by cclub cannot be summarized by this or any list. When I joined a year ago, I had no idea what to expect, and having been involved I can say easily that this is the best thing that I've been involved with since I joined WMU. The rate at which I learned by being surrounded by engaged, knowledgeable peers is incomparable to anything I've experienced in any of my classes. Its my personal goal to make your time at WMU as meaningful as mine. Hope to meet you, - Flay
