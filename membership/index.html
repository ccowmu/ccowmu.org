Membership

Just head down to Kohrman Hall, Room 2225 during office hours or on any Thursday at 6pm. A one-year membership costs $10 and gets you:

  • Awesome Job Networking
  • Standing invite to compete on our CTF Team
  • An account on our Club server, complete with SSH privileges
  • Discounts on entry to PLAN
  • Access to our tools, servers, and educational resources
  • Web hosting

What is really offered by cclub cannot be summarized by this or any list. When I joined I had pretty low expectations, and having been involved I can say easily, and honestly, that this is the best thing that I've been involved with since I came to WMU.

The rate at which I learned by being surrounded by engaged, knowledgeable peers is incomparable to anything I've experienced in any of my classes and I often hear the same from my peers.

Its my personal goal to make your time at WMU as meaningful as mine has been as a result of this organization.

Hope to meet you,
