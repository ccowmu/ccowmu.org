Why Join the Computer Club?

With our own office and the resources in it, our greatest resource remains our people. After almost 40 years at WMU, we've gained hundreds of great members doing remarkable things at companies like Google, Apple, LinkedIn, IBM, and the DoD.

If you're even a little interested in security, programming, hardware, electronics, or have an idea for your own project, then chances are you'll find a friend in us.

The best way to see if we're the right fit is stopping by a meeting to see for yourself. Check out our meeting minutes to see what a typical meeting might entail.
