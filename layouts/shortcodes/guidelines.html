Community Guidelines

Our chat is pretty simple to navigate. Follow these three rules and you'll be golden:

1. Don't be a jerk

Treat everyone with basic respect. No harassment, discrimination, or being unnecessarily hostile.

2. Use common sense

Post in appropriate channels, don't spam, and think before you type. If it seems like a bad idea, it probably is.

3. Officers have final say

Club officers make the final decisions on disputes, channel management, and enforcement. Their word is law.
